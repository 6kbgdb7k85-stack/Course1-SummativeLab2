3 Cat Breeds

Black Cat

A type of cat known for its solid black coat. It is more a monicer for all cat breed with a solid black coat as opposed to a breed itself. Historically black cats have been unfairly treated in the western wold due to associations in folklore with bad luck and witchcraft. Sometimes black cats are nicknamed "house panthers" or simply "void".

Turkish Van

The Turkish Van is standardized-breed first bred in the UK during the '50s from cats obtained from various cities in modern-day Turkey, particularly in the southeastern regions of the country. It is a rare breed known for the distinct fur pattern where only the head and tail are colored with the rest of the cat being with. They have blue or orange eyes and sometimes on eye of each color.
